Identity

The end product is to create a window shade that automatically calibrates to reduce the amount of direct sunlight entering through the window.

Problem

Sunlight that directly hits the inside of the house heats up the inside of the house, therefore increasing costs of cooling down the house through air conditioning.

Solution

Window shades that automatically calibrate to reduce the amount of direct sunlight thus reduces the energy costs of cooling down the area.

Target Market

The target market for this product will be the middle and higher class individual who have enough income to spend on a convenience, and not a necessity.

Competition

There is little competition for this market, and due to this we are able to undercut the market average by roughly 40%.

Revenue Streams

The main revenue stream for the company will be the sale of these window shades.

Marketing Activities

Our marketing will target individuals who wish to reduce their environmental impact as the product reduces energy needed, and will be done via social media. Word of mouth will also be an effective source of marketing.

Expenses

Expenses will include the creation and delivery of the product to the consumers. Other expenses include marketing costs and employees.

Team and Key Roles

The team will comprise of roughly 20 individuals. Key roles will be the CEO, quality assurance manager, manager of marketing, and manager of production.
