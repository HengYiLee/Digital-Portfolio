Software Development Engineer

Software Development Engineers create, design, and test software which solves issues required by others. They regularly debug code and improve it based on the requirements necessary. Also, they commonly work in teams to handle larger tasks more efficiently, and work with other teams such as user experience and quality assurance to produce a seemless product. I believe I would be a good role for this position as I am adept at coding, and am able to easily work in teams and with other teams.

Quality Assurance Engineer

A Quality Assurance Engineer is responsible for ensuring that the finished product meets the necessary requirements and quality of the product requested. Quality Assurance Engineers commonly execute the tasks that are required of the product, and analyse the results. They then improve the quality of the product if possible. I believe I would be a good role for this position as I have done this in the past, and have already learned how to effectively fulfill the requirements of this position.

Project Manager

A Project Manager plans, manages, and organizes resources to complete the creation of a product that is satisfactory for the requirements needed. They commonly plan the schedule and timeline of the project, and facilitate communication between different teams. Also, they provide leadership for the teams, and ensure the completion of a suitable product within the quality that is required. I believe I would make a decent role for this position as I am able to handle logistics relatively well, but lack leadership skills.

Product Manager

A Product Manager ensure the success of a product, and hold the vision for the finished product, and the removal of obstacles that negatively impact that production of the product. They commonly work with other teams to create the product, and understand the requirements which determines the priority of what is completed. I believe I would make a mediocre product manager as I am able to complete logistics, but have a difficult time envisioning a complete path of how to complete something on a large scale.
